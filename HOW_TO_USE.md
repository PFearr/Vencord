# How does it work?

To view your categories, click on your gif icon and click Categories.
<img width="528" height="559" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/482e4c2f-bc5d-4e1a-9439-4629fca7b366" />


You can add new categories by clicking on the big button, naming it and pressing add

<img width="499" height="391" alt="Screenshot 2026-05-27 at 10 33 43 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1fb7e5a9-a59e-44fd-a850-7fb84538cfc1" />

Adding gifs to your categories is simple, Go to favorites (Which is populated by your discord favorite gifs), and right click on any gif you want. You should see add category
<img width="466" height="337" alt="Screenshot 2026-05-27 at 10 34 45 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f467ffde-a87a-4419-8204-48517a1254dc" />

Hovering over add category shows all your listed categories that you can move the gif too
<img width="380" height="416" alt="Screenshot 2026-05-27 at 10 34 58 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b9d3ce4c-03f7-4ba5-b807-92d0f3cdf192" />

**A Gif already in a category will show move**
